EisenScript language

Life was like a box of chocolates. Forrest Gump

EisenScript is a programming language designed by Mikael Hvidtfeldt for generating 3d structures. This shows you that even a simple system has a big possibility to generate surprising and complex structures and also would brings a big amazing to you. These structures have been called 'generative' because of having both systematicness and unpredictability in themselves.

This is a javascript implementation for generating in real time in your browser.
